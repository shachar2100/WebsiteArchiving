Crafting a Unique Movie-Themed Pendant

Ridvan had a unique goal—to pleasantly shock his horror movie fanatic partner with a movie themed custom pendant. And what did he dream up? A reverse bear trap! Unusual, right? But for us, it marked the beginning of an incredibly unique piece.

Jewelry Journey

Movie Themed Custom Pendant Design Process

Armed with a reference image and alongside our master designer, Holly, it was time to transform Ridvan’s vision into reality.

Attention to Detail

Recreating a miniature version demands meticulous attention and skill. Ensuring proportions mirror the larger original object is crucial. Certain intricate details can’t be too small, risking structural integrity. Skipping the sketch, we delved straight into 3D design, sculpting this movie-themed pendant step by step.

 

3D digital design model of the pendant

3D digital design model of the pendant

 

Fans of the movie Saw will quickly recognize this 3D silver pendant, inspired by the infamous reverse bear trap from the film. When we revealed the photo-realistic render to Ridvan, his reaction was priceless. Transforming a spontaneous idea into this uniquely cool piece left him genuinely thrilled, recalling the journey from concept to reality with excitement.

photo-realistic image of movie themed custom pendant

The Result

From Concept to Creation

From what started as a half-joking request—a reverse bear trap necklace for a horror-loving partner—unfolded into an incredible reality. Meticulously crafted, this silver 1 inch piece embodies a unique fusion of cinema and adornment. Intricate details bring the menacing trap to life, capturing the essence of edgy elegance. Ideal for those drawn to unconventional beauty, this pendant is a wearable celebration of bold individuality and the transformative power of jewelry to embody even the most unconventional inspirations.

“It’s not just a piece of jewelry; it’s a testament to the creativity and skill of the maker who turned a whimsical idea into a tangible, awesome reality. His face lit up with pure surprise and joy. Every time he wears it, he’s not just wearing a necklace – he’s showcasing a piece of the excitement and thoughtfulness that went into creating something so uniquely cool.” – Ridvan
