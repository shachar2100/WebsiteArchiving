Wear Your Cause: A Wildlife Conservationist Custom Ring

When Jessica set out to create a wedding ring for her fiancé Pete, she knew it had to reflect his deep passion for wildlife conservation. Pete’s dedication to protecting Africa’s iconic species—lions, African wild dogs, hyenas, giraffes, and elephants—deserves more than words. Over the past year, Pete has established a meaningful partnership with Lion Landscapes, working alongside them to further conservation efforts in the Africa. Jessica envisioned a ring that would serve as a constant reminder of the beautiful creatures he works tirelessly to protect, turning his passion into a unique piece of wearable art that tells a story as special as their bond.

A Creative Journey Begins:

The journey of creating such lifelike animals on a ring was no small feat – it’s all about getting the proportions just right. Each animal needed to be realistic and true to life, which meant paying close attention to the small distinguishing details. Carrie, Jessica’s master designer, suggested a 7mm band—meticulously chosen to ensure the animals’ details were visible and the ring was comfortable for Pete to wear. Every decision made was with the intent to harmonize art with functionality.

Perfecting the Layout:

With the list of animals finalized, our task was clear: envision a seamless scene that intertwined each animal around the band. Our team sketched the initial concepts, focusing keenly on proportion and balance. Each creature needed to complement the next, carving a path around the ring that was as striking as it was meaningful.

Design Concept by JewelryThis

From Sketch to Reality: The JewelryThis Design Process

The adventure continued as Jessica’s sketch morphed into a detailed 3D design. The creative process was meticulous, with each animal scrupulously shaped to achieve Jessica’s envisioned embossed look. The replacement of a rhinoceros with a hyena was one of the many thoughtful adjustments made to ensure the ring was a perfect representation of Pete’s passion.

3D model of the ring

Adding Final Touches to the Design:

Once the 3D model was complete, Jessica had the opportunity to view a photo-realistic image of the finished ring design—an important step that allowed for any final tweaks. This gave her the chance to make last-minute adjustments to perfect the design ensuring that every element reflected the unique artistry that made the piece truly special. With these final touches, the design came together beautifully, and the visual of the completed ring was revealed.

 

Mixed highly polished and matte metal finishes creates depth and dimension

 

Bringing the Vision to Life

Cast in 14k yellow gold with a brilliant high-polish finish, the completed ring was a masterpiece. Matte accents provided depth and dimension, emphasizing the intricate details of each animal—a testament to JewelryThis’s expert craftsmanship and dedication to personalization. Every element makes this custom piece a true representation of Pete’s dedication to wildlife conservation.

She had only a few photos of the cherished piece, but Carrie was able to recreate the diamond necklace and setting exactly as it had been, all while staying within Jessica’s budget. It was a testament to the team’s skill and commitment to bringing meaningful designs to life. As Jessica put it, “I will be going to them for absolutely everything and will likely never buy from a fine jewelry store again.”
