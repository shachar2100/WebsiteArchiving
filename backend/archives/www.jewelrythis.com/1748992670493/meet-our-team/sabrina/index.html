Expertise

 I grew up in the jewelry industry with my parents being fine jewelry manufacturers. One could say I was born with a love for jewelry. After a 15 year career in IT, I decided to come back to jewelry and reconnect with my lost love. I obtained my Graduate Pearls and Graduate Gemologist Diploma from the Gemological Institute of America and my Graduate Jeweler Diploma from New Approach School for Jewelers. From Gemstones to Jewelry Creation, I am dedicated to assisting others in creating the jewelry piece of their dreams. I craft dreams into precious realities, making every piece a memorable moment in someone’s life. 

Fondest Jewelrythis Journeys & My Most Treasured Jewelry Piece

Vibrant Emerald
I love all fine jewelry, and this marquise emerald engagement ring really caught my eye. It offers a modern take on a traditional engagement ring with its choice of marquise-shaped stone and a vibrant emerald green pop.
Personal Power Key
 I'm absolutely captivated by its unique interpretation of the classic key shape, and the intricate detailing is stunningly beautiful. The skill required to craft and clean between the highly dimensional, detailed sections is truly impressive. Not only is it beautiful, but it is also so unique to the wearer, which is truly inspiring.
Daddy's Girl
The last piece of jewelry my late father gave me before he passed away is my most treasured jewelry piece. It is a 24k dragon mounted on stainless steel. I was born in the year of the dragon in the Chinese Zodiac, and I was always a “Daddy’s Girl.” I wear it with pride.
