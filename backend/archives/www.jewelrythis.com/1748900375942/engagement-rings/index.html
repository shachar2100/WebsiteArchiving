YOUR TOP 3 QUESTIONS,
ANSWERED

1. Can you help me design my idea?
Absolutely. Take your time with our designers in finding the right design, metal and stone for your unique piece. You can send us pictures, drawings or just describe it, and we will help you bring it to life.
2. How long does it take to make?
Each piece takes an average of 4 weeks to complete and ship. However, we know that some situations call for speed, so let us know if there is a must-meet deadline for your jewelry.
3. How much will it cost? 
Our high-end custom jewelry is created only for you, with zero premade settings or molds. Unlike buying out of the case, you get the option to choose the metal and stones that match your budget. Each piece starts at $500 and we stand by our work. We guarantee you will love it!
