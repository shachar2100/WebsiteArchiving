Bold and full of spirit, this “Long Live Cowgirls” pendant is a tribute to the fearless, independent women who embrace the Western way of life. Crafted in radiant 10kt yellow gold, its striking block lettering stands strong, exuding confidence and charm. Measuring 1.5 inches in length, this statement piece is designed to capture attention, whether worn alone or layered with other favorites. Perfect for those who live with grit and grace, it’s a wearable celebration of the cowgirl spirit that never fades.
