Earrings

The earring is a decorative piece of jewelry that is worn on the ear, usually the ear lobe, and either hangs from a hole in the earlobe or is attached by a clip. Earrings usually come as a pair, but can be worn in just one ear as well. Earrings have long been a fashion staple and come in a variety of styles for both women and men.
