3 Prioritize Relevance

Prioritizing keyword relevance is crucial for effective SEM. Focus on terms that closely match your product or service offering, as these will drive more qualified traffic and lead to better conversion rates. Avoid the temptation to bid on broad or generic terms simply because they have high search volumes; relevance should always trump volume when it comes to selecting the right keywords for your campaigns.

4 Group Keywords

Organizing your keywords into tightly themed groups can significantly enhance the effectiveness of your SEM campaigns. This not only helps in creating more targeted ad groups and ads but also improves Quality Score in platforms like Google Ads. A higher Quality Score can lead to lower cost-per-clicks and better ad positions, making your SEM efforts more cost-effective and time-efficient.
