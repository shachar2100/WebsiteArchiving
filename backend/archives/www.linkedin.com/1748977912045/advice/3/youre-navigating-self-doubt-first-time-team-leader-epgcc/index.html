1 Embrace Vulnerability

Embracing vulnerability is a powerful step in overcoming self-doubt. Admitting that you don't have all the answers can paradoxically strengthen your team's trust in you. By being open about the learning curve you're experiencing, you invite your employees to contribute their expertise and collaborate on solutions. This creates a culture of mutual respect and continuous learning, where everyone feels valued and confident in their contributions.
