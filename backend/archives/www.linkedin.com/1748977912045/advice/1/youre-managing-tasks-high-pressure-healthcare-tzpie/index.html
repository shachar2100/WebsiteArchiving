You're managing tasks in a high-pressure healthcare setting. How do you decide what comes first?

Managing tasks in a high-pressure healthcare setting requires quick, effective prioritization to ensure patient safety and efficiency.

In a busy healthcare environment, determining which tasks come first is crucial. Here are some strategies to help you prioritize effectively:

  • Assess patient acuity: Focus on patients with the most critical needs first.

  • Use triage protocols: Follow established guidelines to categorize tasks based on urgency.

  • Delegate appropriately: Assign duties to team members based on their skills and availability.

How do you prioritize tasks in a high-pressure healthcare setting?
