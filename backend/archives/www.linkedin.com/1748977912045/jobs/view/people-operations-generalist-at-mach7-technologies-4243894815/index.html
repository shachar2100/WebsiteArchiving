      Kelly Kimball

      Kelly Kimball

      VP, People & Culture

      Summary:


      We're on a mission to reimagine People & Culture as a proactive, strategic partner, not just an administrative function. As our People Operations Generalist, you'll be at the heart of shaping the employee experience at every step of the journey. This is more than HR support, it’s a hands-on opportunity to help build process, culture, and an employee brand in a fast-moving, people-first environment.


      We’re looking for someone who thrives on bringing order to chaos, loves designing thoughtful experiences, and can balance fun with follow-through. You'll work across the entire employee lifecycle, from onboarding and compliance to employee engagement and people programs, supporting employees across the U.S., Canada, and APAC Region.


      This is a high-impact, visible role for a self-starter who’s ready to take initiative, build from scratch, and help drive a vibrant, forward-thinking culture.


      Key Responsibilities:


      Employee Experience & Culture

      • Collaborate on culture-building programs that bring our values to life, from creative onboarding to offboarding that leaves a lasting impression.
      • Help shape our internal employer brand through recognition programs, storytelling, and fun employee events.
      • Partner on employee engagement surveys, stay interviews, and employee feedback loops, then take part in creating real-time action plans.
      • Bring ideas to life: culture campaigns, wellness initiatives, and moments of connection across geographies.

      Process & Program Design

      • Co-create and iterate scalable, modern People processes (onboarding, offboarding, leave management, etc.) with a focus on clarity, inclusivity, and experience.
      • Support the rollout of HR tools, policies, and resources that simplify and elevate the employee journey.
      • Play a key role in launching or optimizing programs like performance reviews, career development, and internal communications.

      Core People Ops Functions

      • Maintain accurate and confidential employee data in HRIS and support reporting needs.
      • Coordinate benefits enrollment, wellness communications, and act as the first line of support for employee benefit questions.
      • Support payroll coordination by partnering with Finance and responding to employee inquiries.
      • Ensure compliance with evolving U.S. and Canadian labor laws, including leave administration and documentation management.

      Recruitment Support

      • Partner with hiring teams to support seamless candidate experiences through scheduling, communication, and offer processes.
      • Help elevate our talent brand with thoughtful candidate touchpoints and recruitment resources.

      What We’re Looking For

      • 3–5 years of progressive HR experience with exposure to multiple functions, bonus points if you’ve helped scale a startup or evolving culture.
      • Passion for building and improving people programs with a creative and proactive mindset.
      • Strong organizational skills and the ability to manage multiple priorities independently.
      • Knowledge of U.S. and Canadian employment law (APAC Region is a bonus, not a must).
      • Proficiency in HRIS platforms (we will be implementing one, your voice will matter).
      • Comfort with ambiguity and a high degree of initiative, you don’t wait to be told what’s broken; you offer solutions.
      • Excellent communication and people skills. You’re empathetic, collaborative, and thrive in a people-first environment.

      Why Join Us?

      • You’ll be part of a culture-first, mission-driven team that values initiative, innovation, and authenticity.
      • You’ll help shape the future of how People & Culture operates in a modern organization.
      • You’ll have a chance to leave your fingerprint on programs, process, and culture from the ground up.
