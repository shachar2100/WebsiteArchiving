      Detailer/Designer

      Department: Engineering

      Reports To: Director of Engineering

      FLSA Status: Hourly

      Location: Lansing, MI

      The Company

      Invio Automation is an Industry 4.0 automation leader that serves a variety of high-growth end-markets including data center and power management, renewable energy, mobility, logistics & fulfillment, and heavy industry. Our collaborative teams deliver mission-critical R&D pre-validation services that lay the foundation for a full spectrum of automation solutions—where precision, efficiency, and accuracy are key to success. With six locations across North America and a global network of collaborative partners, Invio Automation’s geographic footprint provides customers with a presence in key markets where local service and support are valued.

      Invio Automation specializes in single-station robotic cells, multi-station fabrication & assembly lines, bonding & welding technologies, vision systems, and packaging processes. Application capabilities include thermal bonding, web handling, force plotting, heat staking, high-viscosity pumps, precision dispensing, impulse bag sealing, needle bending, highly differentiated Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for heavyweight applications, turnkey assembly lines, welding automation, and custom lift-assist and secure tools for safety-critical applications.

      Description

      Our design team collaborates with the world's largest companies to transform the way things are made. Having a passion for examining “the way it’s always been done” and seeking out a new and better way is a leadership trait we value. This role requires a working knowledge of mechanical principles and an understanding of 3D models, layout drawings, and engineering specifications. Detailers work with Design Engineering to ensure drawings are released to the shop on time and with accuracy.

      Essential Duties And Responsibilities

      Complete 2D drawings and layouts for a variety of moderately complex to complex ergonomic and automated tools, including welding fixtures, end-of-arm tooling, automation holding fixtures, prototype tooling, and general assembly tooling.

      Review 3D models for safety concerns, accuracy, and completion.

      Work concurrently on one or more projects of moderately complex scope.

      Collaborate as a productive member on large projects.

      Schedule activities to complete assigned tasks and projects, communicate and document requisite information per established Invio Automation policies and procedures.

      Understand customer needs and apply technical knowledge to identify drawings.

      Demonstrate Engineering professionalism in all activities and provide efficient service to customers.

      Complete release packages, including entering project information into our ERP system.

      Qualifications:

      To perform this job successfully, an individual must be professional, proactive, and positive in interactions with internal and external customers and coworkers. The requirements listed below represent the knowledge, skills, and/or abilities required.

      Other Skills and Abilities:

      Strong knowledge of SolidWorks required.

      Strong interpersonal skills.

      Good verbal and written skills.

      Proven organizational skills.

      Ability to prioritize and work with limited supervision.

      Good presentation skills.

      Ability to collaborate effectively with a team.

      Ability to explain how and why specific conclusions were reached.

      Ability to maintain confidentiality.

      Ability to work well under pressure and to remain flexible to changing schedules and demands.

      Ability to learn, understand, and apply new techniques and technologies.

      Must be detail-oriented.

      EDUCATION

      Associate degree in CAD, drafting, and/or engineered preferred. Requires three years of mechanical 2D detailing and 3D solid modeling experience and proficiency in SolidWorks. NX and/or AutoCAD knowledge a plus. Must have basic knowledge and expertise in Microsoft Office.

      INVIO AUTOMATION’S CORE VALUES

      We take pride in the reliability of our team and the difficult problems we solve for customers.

      We deliver real customer value that positively impacts the lives of those around us.

      We are dependable. Relationships and trust matter.

      Our ingenuity and desire to improve lives will complement our suite of solutions.

      We strive to demonstrate that our attention to detail does not start or end with the solutions we engineer and deliver.

      We believe exceptional service is better demonstrated than said.
